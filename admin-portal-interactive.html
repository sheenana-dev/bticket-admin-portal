Merchant Agreement
The B-ticket platform agreement that the merchant must accept.

B-ticket Merchant Agreement

This Merchant Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between B-ticket Inc. ("B-ticket") and the Merchant ("Merchant") who registers on the B-ticket platform.

1. Platform Usage

B-ticket provides a digital coupon marketplace platform. Merchants may list their business, create digital coupons, and participate in promotional activities through the platform. There are no listing fees or commissions charged to the merchant.

2. Merchant Obligations

The Merchant agrees to: (a) provide accurate business information, (b) honor all published coupons and their terms, (c) maintain quality standards as outlined by B-ticket, (d) comply with all applicable Philippine laws and regulations.

3. Content Standards

All content submitted (photos, descriptions, coupon details) must be accurate, non-misleading, and comply with B-ticket's content guidelines. B-ticket reserves the right to reject or remove content that does not meet these standards.

4. Term and Termination

This agreement is effective upon approval and continues until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice.

The agreement will be sent to the merchant for digital signature via email after their account is created.
