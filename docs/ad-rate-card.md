## Ad Format Inventory

B-Ticket offers four in-app advertising placements designed to maximize visibility and engagement while preserving user experience.

### Format Specifications

| Format | Size (px) | Placement | Visibility | Best For |
|--------|-----------|-----------|------------|----------|
| Hero Banner | 357 × 96 | Home Screen | 100% of home screen visitors | Brand awareness, new merchant promos |
| Top Banner | 392 × 72 | Explore / Map | Visible during active browsing | Location-based deals, event promotions |
| Pop-up | 310 × 371 | Overlay (any screen) | Full attention, dismissible | Flash sales, limited offers, app features |
| Full-Screen | 392 × 615 | Interstitial (5s skip) | Maximum impact, 5-second guaranteed | Grand openings, premium campaigns |

### Display Behavior & Screen Time

| Format | Display Behavior | Screen Time | Close / Skip | Frequency Cap |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| Hero Banner | Persistent; rotates every 5s between merchants | 5s per rotation (time-based switch) | No close button (native placement) | Max 15 merchants; 1 ad per 5s cycle |
| Top Banner | Persistent; rotates every 5s between merchants on scroll | 5s per rotation (time-based switch) | No close button (native placement) | Max 10 merchants; 1 ad per 5s cycle |
| Pop-up | Appears on app open or screen transition; overlay with dimmed BG | **3 seconds** minimum (mandatory view) before close enabled | ✕ Close button appears after 3s | Max 5 merchants; 1× per user/session |
| Full-Screen | Interstitial between screen transitions; full device takeover | **5 seconds** minimum (mandatory view) before skip enabled | Skip Ad button after 5s | Max 3 merchants; 1× per user/day |

### How Screen Time Translates to Merchant Value

- **Hero Banner:** Rotates every 5 seconds between up to 15 merchants. In a typical 45-second Home session, a merchant's ad appears approximately 3 times (if 15 merchants) to 9 times (if 5 merchants). At [X] daily active users, this delivers [X × 3–9] daily impressions per merchant.
- **Top Banner:** Same 5-second rotation with up to 10 merchants. Users browsing Explore/Map for 30 seconds see each merchant's ad approximately 3–6 times. Location-based browsing means high-intent visibility during the deal discovery moment.
- **Pop-up:** The 3-second mandatory view ensures the ad is seen and read before dismissal. Industry data shows 3 seconds is the sweet spot — shorter feels too aggressive, longer increases uninstall rates. At 1× per session, this protects UX while guaranteeing impressions.
- **Full-Screen:** The 5-second mandatory view is the industry standard (same as YouTube pre-roll and GrabAds interstitials). Users must engage with the creative before skipping, making this the highest-impact format for grand openings and premium campaigns.

## Pricing: Flat Monthly Rate

B-Ticket's advertising model is simple: merchants pay a fixed monthly fee for an ad placement slot. Month-to-month only — no annual contracts. Hard caps on merchant slots ensure each advertiser gets meaningful visibility. Additional pricing models (CPC, CPO) may be introduced in future phases based on platform growth.

### Monthly Rates & Slot Caps

All prices in Philippine Pesos (₱).

| Format | Monthly Rate | Max Slots | Rotation | Revenue Cap | Contract |
|--------|-------------|-----------|----------|-------------|----------|
| Hero Banner | **₱15,000/mo** | **15** | 5s time-based | **₱225,000** | Monthly |
| Top Banner | **₱8,000/mo** | **10** | 5s time-based | **₱80,000** | Monthly |
| Pop-up | **₱20,000/mo** | **5** | Per-user rotation | **₱100,000** | Monthly |
| Full-Screen | **₱25,000/mo** | **3** | Per-user rotation | **₱75,000** | Monthly |

- Total maximum Flat Rate revenue: **₱480,000/month** across 33 merchant slots
- No annual or multi-month contracts — merchants subscribe monthly and can cancel before the next cycle
- Pricing is reviewed monthly based on demand; rates may increase once slots fill up
- Hero & Top Banner: 5-second time-based rotation — ad switches to next merchant every 5 seconds
- Pop-up & Full-Screen: per-user rotation — each user sees one merchant's ad per session/day; next user sees the next merchant
- When a slot hits max capacity, placement is marked "SOLD OUT" until a merchant cancels or admin raises the cap

### Slot Capacity & Rotation Summary

Each placement has a hard cap on the number of concurrent merchants. Slot caps protect ad value and can be raised by admin as platform traffic grows.

| Placement | Max Slots | Rotation Type | Revenue Cap | Sold-Out Behavior |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Hero (Home) | **15** | 5s time-based | ₱225,000/mo | Blocked + waitlist |
| Top (Explore) | **10** | 5s time-based | ₱80,000/mo | Blocked + waitlist |
| Pop-up | **5** | Per-user rotation | ₱100,000/mo | Blocked + waitlist |
| Full-Screen | **3** | Per-user rotation | ₱75,000/mo | Blocked + waitlist |

*Total maximum Flat Rate revenue at full capacity: ₱480,000/month across 33 merchant slots. Once a placement is full, new merchants are directed to other available slots or placed on a waitlist.*

*Slot caps are introductory and may be raised as platform traffic grows. Admin can adjust caps via the pricing control panel.*

## Terms & Specifications

### Creative Specifications

- Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, GIF (animated allowed for Pop-up and Full-Screen only)
- Maximum file size: 500KB (banners), 1MB (Pop-up), 2MB (Full-Screen)
- Creative assets must be submitted at least 3 business days before campaign start
- B-Ticket reserves the right to reject creatives that violate community guidelines or degrade user experience
- Advertiser is responsible for ensuring all content complies with Philippine advertising regulations

### Booking & Payment Terms

- 100% payment upfront before campaign month begins
- Auto-renews monthly unless cancelled 5 days before next cycle
- Cancellation: non-refundable once campaign is live; cancellations before launch receive 80% refund
- Payment via: GCash, Maya, B-Ticket Wallet, or Credit/Debit Card (via Xendit)
- Frequency capping: Pop-up limited to 1/user/session; Full-Screen limited to 1/user/day
- Reporting: monthly performance report (impressions, taps, CTR) via email and merchant dashboard

### Placement Rotation & Capacity

All placements rotate across merchants within fixed slot caps: Hero Banner (max 15, 5s time-based rotation), Top Banner (max 10, 5s time-based rotation), Pop-up (max 5, per-user rotation), Full-Screen (max 3, per-user rotation). When a slot is full, it displays "SOLD OUT" in the merchant portal until a merchant cancels or admin raises the cap.
